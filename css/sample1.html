Composition in Photography

Composition is one of the key elements of a great photograph. Using techniques like the rule of thirds, leading lines, and framing, photographers can create visually appealing and well-balanced shots. Paying attention to subject placement and background elements enhances the storytelling in an image.

Importance of Lighting

Lighting plays a crucial role in photography. Natural light, golden hour (sunrise and sunset), and artificial lighting setups can dramatically impact the mood of a photo. Understanding how to use shadows, reflections, and exposure can help capture stunning visuals.

Photo Editing & Post-Processing

Post-processing is an essential step in modern photography. Using tools like Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop, photographers enhance colors, adjust exposure, and refine details to create a polished final image. Editing helps bring out the best in raw photos while maintaining a natural look.
